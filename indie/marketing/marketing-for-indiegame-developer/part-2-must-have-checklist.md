# Marketing for Indiegame Developer 
# Part 2: Must-Have Checklist

## Internal

* Websites
    - Company website
    - Game website (optional)
    - Game landing page
* Development blog
    - social buttons (1+, like, etc.)
    - minimum weekly posts
* Social media
    - Facebook
        + Company page
        + Game page
    - Twitter
        + Company account
        + Game account (optional)
* Trailer
    - 1 min long
    - sound + music
    - show gameplay
    - update with each milestone    
* Demo
* RSS Feed

* Marketing strategy
